How it works
Embarking on this testing journey is simple, designed with you in mind. Here's a step-by-step guide to what you/ll personally experience:
01.
Get your .Test Kit
Upon ordering, you'll receive a specially designed test kit delivered directly to your doorstep. Every kit is meticulously packed with all the necessary items you'll need.
02.
Take your test
Using the provided materials in the kit, collect your sample. Ensure you follow the step-by-step instructions carefully to ensure accuracy. Once done, safely package the sample in the return envelope.
03.
Test Analysis
After sending your sample back, our state-of-the-art laboratory undertakes a detailed analysis. Our dedicated team of professionals ensures that every sample undergoes rigorous testing.
04.
Get your Result
No more prolonged waiting periods! As soon as your test has been analyzed, you'll receive your results. We pride ourselves on our swift turnaround times, ensuring that you're kept informed..
